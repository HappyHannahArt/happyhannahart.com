About

Hey there, I'm Leslie the owner of Happy Hannah Art! My business is individually owned and operated out of Northern California, so I pretty much try to do it all (for now)! I'm an artist and sticker enthusiast who loves to draw silly animals and fun snacks! As a '90s kid, I admired all of the brands that celebrated stickers and bright stationery that still inspire me today.

Happy Hannah Art's mission is to spread fun and positivity through stationery products. What better way to make someone smile than to surprise them with a sticker or greeting card? My business is named after my dog Hannah who is the star of my previous sticker club that included a Hannah-narrated newsletter and newly-designed stickers that were sent to family and friends. I hope my art helps send a bit more sunshine your way!
